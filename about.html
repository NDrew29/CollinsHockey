Meet Mike Collins

Former pro forward. Dedicated teacher. North Shore hockey mentor.

Coaching Philosophy

Mike blends NHL-caliber habits with youth-friendly coaching language. Every session is tied to a measurable outcome: better puck decisions, faster transitions, stronger skating mechanics, and leadership growth. Last Shift Hockey is built for players in Manchester-by-the-Sea and throughout the North Shore who want a trusted mentor invested in their long-term development.

Career Snapshot

  • 2-time BCHL Champion with Vernon Vipers.
  • NCAA (East) Second All-American and Hockey East All-Star.
  • 5 seasons in the German DEL with Krefeld Pinguine and ERC Ingolstadt.
  • DEL2 scoring leader (81 points) with EC Kassel Huskies.
